Capabilities

Six things your professor
notices but won't tell you.

Filler words

Real-time detection of um, uh, like, and you know. Stop counting on your fingers.

Speaking pace

We flag when you drift outside the ideal 120–150 wpm. Keep your audience engaged.

Head position

We measure tilt, forward lean, and rotation. Keep your head level and engaged with the room.

Eye contact

We track your iris position to see if you're looking at the camera — not at your notes.

Facial expression

Smile detection via lip corner tracking. Presence isn't just words — it's how you look saying them.

Blink rate

Too few blinks = staring (uncomfortable). Too many = nervous. We find your natural rhythm.
